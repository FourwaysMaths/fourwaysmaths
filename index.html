About Fourways Maths

Fourways Maths was founded in the heart of Fourways with a simple mission: to help learners unlock their mathematical potential through high-quality, personalised support. What began as a local tutoring service has since expanded beyond Fourways, with learners joining our online lessons from across South Africa.

We believe that every learner can succeed in Mathematics and Mathematical Literacy when given the right guidance, encouragement, and practice. Our lessons focus on building strong foundations, improving confidence, and developing the problem-solving skills needed for long-term academic success.

Fourways Maths supports learners across multiple curricula, including CAPS and IEB (Grades 8–12), and Cambridge (Grades 8–11). This allows us to provide targeted, curriculum-specific guidance while maintaining the personalised approach that has become the foundation of our teaching philosophy.

Whether a learner is aiming to pass, improve their marks, or achieve distinctions, Fourways Maths is committed to providing professional, affordable tutoring that delivers real results.
